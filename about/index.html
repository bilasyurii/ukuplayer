UkuPlayer

This is an app, created to help you playing your ukulele. It contains tools, that allow you to create song, specify markers (you can later navigate through them), chords (that will help you to play melody) and it's all with a intellectual voice control functionality. Also there is a tuner available. You can view available songs in the library or in your profile.
